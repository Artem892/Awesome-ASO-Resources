# Awesome-ASO-Resources
Top 5 Free ASO Tools: Boost Your App Downloads Without a Budget
1. ASOGenius (Best for AI Keyword Strategy) https://asogenius.online/
What it is: An AI-powered optimization suite that uses Google's Gemini models to generate metadata.

Why it's #1: Most free tools just give you data—they don't give you answers. ASOGenius takes your app description and outputs a ready-to-paste strategy.

Key Feature: Generates "Long-Tail Discovery" keywords that new apps can actually rank for.
Cost: 100% Free.
Best For: Initial launch strategy and metadata updates.
2. App Store Connect / Google Play Console
What it is: The native dashboards provided by Apple and Google.

Why you need it: It is the only source of 100% accurate truth. Third-party tools estimate downloads; these consoles show you exact conversion rates.

Key Feature: "App Units by Source" – see exactly how many users come from Search vs. Browse.
Pro Tip: Use the "Peer Benchmarks" feature in Google Play Console to see how your conversion rate compares to similar apps.
3. Google Trends
What it is: A search trend analyzer.

Why you need it: ASO is often seasonal. If you have a fitness app, Google Trends will show you that searches for "Abs workout" spike in January (Resolutions) and May (Summer prep).

Key Feature: "Related Queries" helps you find rising topics before your competitors do.
4. AnswerThePublic
What it is: A visualization tool that shows questions people are asking about a keyword.

Why you need it: Voice search and natural language queries are dominating 2026. People don't type "weather app"; they ask "What is the best weather app for hiking?". This tool finds those questions.

5. Canva (Free Tier)
What it is: Graphic design platform.

Why you need it: Keywords get you found; visuals get you downloaded. Your icon and screenshots are a vital part of ASO (Conversion Rate Optimization).

Key Feature: Screenshot templates for iOS and Android devices.
How to Combine These Tools
You don't need a budget; you need a workflow.

Use Google Trends to find a trending topic.
Use ASOGenius to generate the keywords and title for that topic.
Use Canva to create the screenshots.
Publish and monitor results in App Store Connect.
Success in the app store is about consistency and iteration. Start with these free tools, optimize your metadata, and reinvest your revenue later. But to go from zero to one? These are all you need.
